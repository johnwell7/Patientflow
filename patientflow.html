Built for PHI from day one.
Not an afterthought.

We treat patient data the way the law demands and the way your patients deserve. Every architectural decision in PatientFlow AI is filtered through HIPAA compliance first.

No PHI ever touches an environment without a signed BAA. No patient name ever reaches an AI model. No tool enters the stack without compliance verification. This isn't a feature. It's the foundation.
